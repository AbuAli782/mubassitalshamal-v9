About Us

At Mubassit Al-Shamal Store, we believe that construction is not just a building process, but an art and a fundamental pillar for creating a bright future. We are one of the leading stores specialized in providing essential building materials in Riyadh, offering a diverse range of products including cement, blocks, sand, gravel, and many other building materials that meet your project needs, whether you are a contractor or on a journey to build your personal home.

Our Vision

To be the ideal partner for the success of construction projects, by providing high-quality products and distinguished services that elevate our customers' expectations. Our success depends on a professional team that possesses the experience and passion to ensure providing the best customer experience, in addition to our commitment to keeping up with the latest quality and innovation standards in the construction field.

Our Mission

We strive with dedication and diligence to provide distinguished services. Our goal is excellence in every step we take, from providing reliable products to leaving a mark that makes us the first choice for customers seeking quality, professionalism, and added services.

Our Goal

At Mubassit, we place customer satisfaction at the top of our priorities by providing an outstanding shopping experience that contributes to the success of their projects. To achieve this, we focus on:

  • Providing High-Quality Products To ensure project sustainability and success
  • Exceptional Customer Service That meets and exceeds your expectations
  • Ease and Flexibility In the shopping and ordering process
  • Fast and Safe Delivery Directly to your project site
  • Innovation and Sustainability In providing advanced construction solutions

Mubassit is not just a store, but your partner in building your dreams. Let us help you turn your ideas into a successful and sustainable reality.
